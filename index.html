The Expertise Behind Our Traditional Indian Dishes

Bringing traditional Indian dishes to your table requires more than just ingredients — it demands expertise, experience, and a deep understanding of the culinary arts. Our chefs have honed their skills through years of practice and learning, mastering the art of blending spices, balancing flavors, and cooking techniques that preserve the essence of Indian cuisine.

Exploring Popular Traditional Indian Dishes on Our Menu

Let’s take a closer look at some of the traditional Indian dishes you’ll find at Top of India and what makes them special:

  • Butter Chicken: A beloved North Indian dish, butter chicken features tender chicken cooked in a creamy tomato-based sauce enriched with butter and aromatic spices. Its mild yet flavorful profile makes it a favorite among many.
  • Chana Masala: A hearty vegetarian dish made with chickpeas simmered in a spicy tomato gravy. It’s a great example of how Indian cuisine creates satisfying meat-free meals without sacrificing taste.
  • Lamb Rogan Josh: Originating from Kashmir, this dish highlights slow-cooked lamb in a rich blend of spices that include Kashmiri red chili, giving it a deep red color and robust flavor.
  • Saag Paneer: Fresh spinach cooked with cubes of paneer cheese, spiced delicately to maintain a balance of creaminess and earthiness.
  • Biryani: A fragrant rice dish layered with meat or vegetables and cooked with saffron, cardamom, and other spices. Biryani is a celebratory dish that showcases Indian culinary complexity.

Each of these dishes carries a story and a tradition, and we take great care to prepare them as authentically as possible.
